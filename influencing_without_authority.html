Influencing Without Authority

PMs are not CEOs, they just manage the product. They do not have authority on people like CEO, so how to get things done?
Here's a list of things that might help.

  • Build Relationships
    - Build trust and credibility
    - Share credit for success
    - Organise team bonding activities
  • Align Incentives
    - Every stakeholder has different incentives
    - Find common ground between the stakeholders, can help you to align incentives
    - Involve your stakeholders early on
  • Share your inputs
    - Inputs and factors that impact the decision making with the team so that all work towards that
    - This can include user research metrics, and tradeoffs between product impact and risk
  • Escalate up
    - If stakeholders do not align, Escalate up
    - Top down approach from execs can help tie break situations
  • Be creative
    - Organise a design sprint with all of your key decision makers
    - Convince others to make part time contributions to your project
