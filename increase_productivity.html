Increase Productivity at Workplace

Was going through an article which discussed measures to increase productivity at workplace.
Here's a summary for you all :

  • Create smaller pull requests. Never leave a day's work without checking it in.
  • Never check-in code that does not compile or breaks the build.
  • Have a clear understanding of the feature before you start to implement it. Ask as many questions as you want to have a clear idea of the requirement.
  • Keep your code simple and well structured. Your teammates should be able to read the code and understand the intended use of it.
  • Focus on shipping small. Break your assignments into smaller logical chunks. Get it clarified and then deliver the smallest possible block of working functionality as requested.
  • Keep a positive attitude towards every feedback.
  • Limit your personal tasks during office hours to a bare minimum. You can always take permission from the manager if you have to take a couple of hours off to attend to unavoidable personal issues.
  • Spend your time and effort to learn technologies that you can actually use to implement something in your workplace.
  • Use the learnings. You will learn more from implementing something on your own than following any tutorial.
