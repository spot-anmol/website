Tips & Tricks Beyond Textbooks

Here's a list of Tips & Tricks Beyond Textbooks by Microsoft Product Leader : Vishal Anil
Take a look

  • PEOPLE
    - Be Selfless
    - Be Relentless and don't have ego
    - You need to have your engineering Managers back
    - Identify the primary stakeholders and get their buy in first
    - Your opinion vs My opinion vs Customer input + data
    - Anchor customers : who define MVP scope. keep in touch after MVP as well
  • PROCESS First 1-3 Months
    - Attend every standup and meeting you can possibly can
    - Make list of all acronymns you hear
    - Have 1:1 with every single PM and Engineer : Passionate about them
    - Choose 4 things you will do in a day
    - Retrospectives, planning & bringing the team together : Setup scenario reviews
    - Quaterly compete analysis : check competition, keep a pulse of them
  • TECHNOLOGY
    - Study architectures and engineering specs : Helps you think on how to create value for customer
    - Learn about UI/UX : Interaction, Visual, User Reseach, Scenarios, Typography, Layouts
    - Book : Don't make me think
    - The Day 1 experience : zero friction on day one
    - Notice trends and patterns
    - Taking risk : Learn from failures
