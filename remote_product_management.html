Remote Product Management

What makes the PM job difficult? Dealing and resolving the ambiguity for all stakeholders. Follow the OCD Framework : Organise => Communicate <=> Delegate
Multiple Stakeholders of the PM are : Analysts, Finance, Operations, Customers Executives, Legal, Tax, Public Policy
In the OCD Framework : Organise => Communicate <=> Delegate Resolving Ambiguity comes under organise And Managing and Convincing Stakeholders and their expectations comes under Communicate and Delegate.

    Organize:
  • 1. Subject Deep Dive
  • 2. Identify Stakeholders
  • 3. Quantitative and Qualitative Data
  • 4. Problem Statement and SMART Goals
  • 5. Assemble a Team
  • 6. Document Storage (SharePoint)
  • 7. Communication Channels (Slack)
  • 8. Vision/Roadmap/BRDs
    Communicate:
  • 1. Chat groups for informal communications
  • 2. Screen Sharing/Video Calling
  • 3. Data Driven Stories
  • 4. Daily/Weekly/Biweekly meetings
  • 5. Regular Status Updates
    Delegate:
  • 1. Develop a delegation plan and align with key POCs, Stakeholders and Leadership
  • 2. Brainstorm with your tech and business teams to break down the roadmap into smaller features
  • 3. Assign ownership of individual components to identify POCs based on alignment
  • 4. Develop a tracker with roles and responsibilities clearly defined
    Remember, the main goals are :
  • Organising for Success
  • Communicating Efficiently
  • Delegating effectively and Continuous Communication
